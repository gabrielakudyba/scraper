The 31st SMM - Shipbuilding, Machinery and Marine Trade Fair presents global developments, technologies, and services shaping the maritime industry across shipbuilding, marine systems, and port operations.

Product & Service Categories
  • Maritime security systems
  • Prime movers and drive systems
  • Ports and port technology
  • Electrical engineering and marine electronics
  • Ship fittings and onboard equipment
  • Marine technology solutions
  • Shipyard installations and production equipment
  • Cargo handling systems and special vessel equipment
  • Auxiliary systems for propulsion
  • Propulsion, manoeuvring, and energy efficiency devices
  • Ship operation and navigation equipment
  • Ship repair, conversion, and retrofit solutions
  • Maritime services
Who should Attend
  • Shipowners and fleet operators
  • Shipyard managers and technical directors
  • Marine equipment manufacturers
  • Port authorities and port operators
  • Maritime engineers and naval architects
  • Shipbrokers and chartering professionals
  • Technology providers for the maritime sector
  • Industry consultants and service providers
  • Media covering maritime and shipping industries
